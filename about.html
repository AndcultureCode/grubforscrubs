Frequently Asked Questions

We’ve shared some of our most frequently asked questions below about the Grub for Scrubs fundraiser campaign. If you can’t find an answer to your question, feel free to contact us.

Question & Answer

  1. What happens when I donate to a restaurant or coffee shop?

    Your donation amount will be added to the restaurant or coffee shop’s total. When that business hits a milestone, including covering the cost for processing fees, the Grub for Scrubs fundraising coordinator will reach out to place an order for delivery. The target milestone amount is based on what the business will be delivering. It is $1,000 for meal delivery and $250 for a coffee or baked goods delivery. The delivery date will be scheduled in coordination with UPMC Pinnacle.

  2. What happens when I donate to the general donation fund?

    While we monitor donation activity to determine how we best support those working towards a goal, we want to use the general donation funds as quickly as we can to place orders for delivery. The process is as follows: Your general donation amount will be added to the general donation total. When the general donation total hits a milestone, including covering the cost for processing fees, the Grub for Scrubs fundraising coordinator will reach out to the next business in the order queue to place an order for delivery. The order queue is managed by the Grub for Scrubs fundraising coordinator, and businesses are added to the order queue based on their onboarding date. The target milestone amount is based on what the business will be delivering. It is $1,000 for meal delivery and $250 for a coffee or baked goods delivery. The delivery date will be scheduled in coordination with UMPC Pinnacle.

    If donations to the campaign slow or if a business is unable to hit a milestone, the general donation fund could be used to help a restaurant or coffee shop hit the target goal to trigger an order. The Grub for Scrubs fundraising coordinators are monitoring the donations to determine if funds should be allocated from the general donation fund to restaurants or coffee shops to help them reach a milestone. The goal is to place orders as quickly and as frequently as we can to make an impact on all involved.

  3. What happens if a restaurant does not hit the target milestone?

    The Grub for Scrubs fundraising coordinators are monitoring the donations to determine if funds should be allocated from the general donation fund to restaurants or coffee shops to help them reach a milestone. The goal is to place orders as quickly and as frequently as we can to make an impact on all involved. If you are a donor or business that is interested in helping a restaurant or coffee shop hit a milestone and you need help through the process, please feel free to use this form to connect the Grub for Scrubs fundraising coordinators to assist.

  4. Who is managing the fundraising campaign?

    Fundraising proceeds are being collected through Pledge It's fundraising platform by Catamaran on behalf of the Grub for Scrubs fundraising campaign. All donations will be managed in collaboration with andculture and will be used to purchase meals at local restaurants or coffee shops to feed healthcare providers at UPMC Pinnacle until the funds have been exhausted. If you have questions regarding the tax-deductibility of this contribution, please consult a tax professional.

  5. How much of my donation goes to placing orders with restaurants and coffee shops?

    Donations are being collected through Pledge It, a Harrisburg-based fundraising platform. The Pledge It platform uses Stripe as the payment processor. While the Pledge It platform is offered to the Grub for Scrubs campaign at no cost, there is a processing fee that is deducted from the donation amount. Stripe’s processing fee is 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction. Catamaran and andculture are offering their support to the Grubs for Scrubs campaign at no cost.

  6. What donation information are you collecting?

    Donations are being collected through Pledge It, a Harrisburg-based fundraising platform, and the Grub for Scrubs campaign coordinators will have access to all donation data via the Pledge It platform, as well as through the payment processor, Stripe. To learn more about the information collected through the donation process, please read Pledge It’s privacy policy here.

  7. I am a restaurant or coffee shop owner, how can I get involved?

    We’d love to get you added to the program. Please complete this form, and a Grub for Scrubs fundraising coordinator will be in contact as quickly as possible.

  8. How long will the fundraising campaign last?

    The goal is to run the Grub for Scrubs fundraiser for as long as we can. We will continue to use the donations to purchase meals at local restaurants to feed healthcare providers at UPMC Pinnacle until all of the donation funds have been used.

  9. Where can I find the total amount raised for the campaign?

    The total amount raised can be found at the top of the website homepage. The total amount includes all donations made to both the general fund and to restaurants or coffee shops. It does not factor in the cost of processing fees.

  10. How many meals are delivered per order?

    Our goal is to deliver enough food, coffee or bakery items for 100 people at UPMC Pinnacle per delivery. The Grub for Scrubs fundraising coordinators are monitoring donation activity and will adjust as needed to ensure we can place orders as quickly and as frequently as we can to make an impact on all involved.

  11. Who is delivering the meals?

    Each restaurant or coffee shop is helping to provide delivery.

  12. What type of meals will be provided?

    The goal is to provide something that is “grab and go” style — such as sandwiches, wraps, coffee, baked goods, etc. The Grub for Scrubs fundraising coordinator is working closely with UPMC Pinnacle to stagger delivery dates and times.
