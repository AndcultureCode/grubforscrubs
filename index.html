Your Donations at Work

The Grub for Scrubs team is working diligently to use every single donation. You may have noticed that some restaurants did not hit their target or were on their way to the next milestone when the campaign ended. And that is okay! We’ll work closely with each restaurant to make sure we can use the dollars donated to place an order. For some, this means they may not donate an entire meal, and that too is okay! Check out the deliveries section of the website over the next few weeks for updates.
