Our Mission

Grub for Scrubs is a fundraising campaign designed to support those who are serving us on the front lines — local restaurants and healthcare providers. The idea is simple — make a donation to the Grub for Scrubs fundraiser, and the donation will be used to place an order with a local restaurant to feed UPMC Pinnacle’s hospital staff.

How It Works

DONATE / Donate to Grubs for Scrubs to help your favorite restaurant or coffee shop reach their milestone. You can also make a general donation that will be used by the fundraiser coordinator to place orders.

PREPARE / Every time your favorite spot hits their milestone amount, we will use the donations to place and order with them.

Deliver / The delivery process will be a coordinated effort with UPMC Pinnacle to ensure we follow hospital policy.

FEED / Each dollar raised will feed staff on a rotating schedule between Harrisburg Hospital and UPMC Pinnacle Community Osteopathic.

Have questions? Visit our FAQs.
